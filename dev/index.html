typenav

Navigate websites with your keyboard


Forms will intentionally block typenav from working.
i.e. Click within this field and type freely and nothing will happen:


But click outside the form and typenav will be re-enabled. So the keyboard will trigger the urls.

Try typing a,b,c or d for an example...


also try quickly typing 'ab' or 'ap' to see how longer keys can be used...
